Welcome to my blog

Posted on 2017-04-04 | In diary

I just setup a new blog on my github. I used to read a lot of stuff about coding, testing and all kinds of books.
But I never recorded them. Sometimes when I had some technical issues and I searched on the internet it turned out these problems were solved by me before. And sometimes I mastered some new tool, new language but they faded after I rarely used them later.

So now I decided to write them down. My blog will have coding, testing and the stories of my family.
